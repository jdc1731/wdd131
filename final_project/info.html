Meet the Owner

I'm Joyce, the proud owner of The Mane Event. Life is never dull around here—with a small herd that includes my beloved horses, donkeys, and a spunky little pony, every day is a new adventure. I’m also a mom to six amazing kids who help keep things lively and full of love. Along with our equines, we share our home with three playful dogs, a curious goat, and a charming pig who thinks he's one of the kids. Animals have always been a huge part of our lives, and I'm so excited to share our journey with you!
